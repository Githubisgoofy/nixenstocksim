Nixen Global Sim v9
The flagship market simulation engine by Lowen LLC.

Nixen Global Sim is a high-performance, minimalist trading environment built for real-time asset management. Operating under the Lowen LLC enterprise suite, this simulator provides a sleek, professional interface for monitoring global equity and executing algorithmic trades.

🌐 Live Deployment
🚀 Core Features
Lowen Analytics Equity Curve: A real-time data visualization tool powered by Chart.js that tracks your total equity and portfolio growth in a high-fidelity line graph.

Dynamic Market Exchange: A live-updating marketplace featuring a variety of sectors:

Technology: High-growth assets like Apple, Nvidia, and Microsoft.

Finance: Global staples including JPMorgan and Visa.

Retail: Consumer giants like Disney, Starbucks, and Walmart.

Automotive: Industry leaders including Tesla and Toyota.

Digital Assets: A dedicated "Digital" tab for Crypto (BTC, ETH, SOL).

Intelligent Trade Ledger: A sophisticated backend that logs every transaction with timestamps and price-point accuracy.

Liquid Capital Management: Track your "Liquid Capital" vs. "Portfolio Assets" to maintain a balanced investment strategy.

Enterprise UI/UX:

Lowen LLC Branding: Proprietary watermarking and "2026 Edition" enterprise styling.

Live News Ticker: Real-time updates on market system status and global trading conditions.

Reactive Search: Instantly filter assets by name or ticker symbol.

System Reset: Securely wipe local data and reset the simulation environment.

🛠️ Technical Specifications
Engine: Vanilla JavaScript (ES6+) with raw algorithmic price volatility.

Visuals: Chart.js for equity rendering and FontAwesome 6 for iconography.

Storage: Local state persistence for continuous portfolio tracking.

Styling: Custom CSS3 variables with a focus on "Inter" typography and high-contrast professional color palettes.

📦 Quick Start
Navigate to nixenstocksim.pages.dev.

Review your starting Liquid Capital.

Use the Market Exchange to search for assets and execute your first trade.

Monitor the Equity Curve to visualize your portfolio performance over time.
